45-year-old Bob Mistro is perhaps best known as an accomplished arranger as well as soloist, devoting most of his time between those two very different worlds of jazz.

Serena Melodius

Serena Melodious, a virtuoso pianist, has been active on the club scene in Omaha and Kansas City for the last twenty years. Melodious is a rarely seen piano genius whose new album, Days of Summer, has been called the first great jazz release of the Millinium.

CD: Days of Summer, Released 2019
