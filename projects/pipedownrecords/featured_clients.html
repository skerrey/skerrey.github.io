Featured Client: Chill Bill
A 37-year-old musician, Chill Bill (William Williams), is a local guitar player in Omaha, Nebraska.
Chill Bill has been playing the guitar for over 20 years and started when he was in high school.
It was in high school where he had learned guitar to be in his school's rock band and loved it ever since.

Chill Bill was apart of several bands since high school.
These bands were Music Heads, The Good Notes, and The Treble Crew.
Bill since then has broken away from bands and started his own solo playing.

Chill Bill's music genre is mostly rock n' roll but has been known to play country blues on occassion.
